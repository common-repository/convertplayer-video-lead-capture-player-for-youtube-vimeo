Turn Any Video into a Highly Effective Opt-in Form

== Description ==

ConvertPlayer is a great way to turn your video viewers in to paying customers. ConvertPlayer helps you turn any video into a highly effective opt-in form. This is official ConvertPlayer WordPress plugin.


== Installation ==
1. Upload and extract plugin package to the "/wp-content/plugins/" directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the "Plugins" menu in WordPress.
3. Inside the plugin add a YouTube or Vimeo video url
4. Insert the shortcode to a WP post and you are good to go!

== Frequently Asked Questions ==
= How do I insert the ConvertPlayer video into my post? =
ConvertPlayer will add a special button inside the WordPress post editor. Simply click on the ConvertPlayer icon in the post editor and pick the video from dropdown. It will add a ConvertPlayer shortcode to your WordPress post or page.

= How do I save my video and changes? =
All changes in your videos are saved immediately as you edit and customize it, so that you never lose any data. No need to save anything.

= How do I use ConvertPlayer with my integration? =
ConvertPlayer currently supports:

*   ActiveCampaign
*   AWeber
*   ConvertKit
*   Drip from LeadPages
*   GetResponse
*   Infusionsoft
*   MailChimp
*   MailerLite
*   Mautic
*   Zapier
*   Email Notification

And many more are coming soon.
You can connect your integation the first time you are adding an Opt-in box to your video.

= How do I customize the text and headline in the optin box? =
Inside the video editor, open or add an video action. And then simply click inside the optin box screen on the text you want to change. A small modal propt will open allowing you to make changes.
